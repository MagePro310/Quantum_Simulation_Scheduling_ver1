FFD (First Fit Decreasing):
  Type: Heuristic
  Strategy: Sort by size, fit to first available machine
  Pros: Fast, simple, good makespan
  Best for: Quick scheduling, small-medium workloads

MTMC (Multi-Tenancy Multi-Cloud):
  Type: Heuristic  
  Strategy: Balance load across multiple machines
  Pros: High utilization, good load balancing
  Best for: Multi-machine environments

MILQ_extend (MILP-based ILQ Extended):
  Type: ILP (Integer Linear Programming)
  Strategy: Optimal task decomposition with cutting
  Pros: Handles large circuits, optimal throughput
  Best for: Complex circuits needing decomposition

NoTaDS (No Task Decomposition Scheduling):
  Type: ILP
  Strategy: Schedule whole circuits without cutting
  Pros: No cutting overhead, simpler execution
  Best for: Circuits fitting machine constraints
